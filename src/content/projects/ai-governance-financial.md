This engagement involved designing an AI governance framework specifically for financial infrastructure operators—entities managing critical payment systems, clearing houses, and digital public infrastructure.

The challenge was threefold: establishing technical guardrails without prescriptive technology mandates, building organizational readiness beyond just AI literacy, and creating coordination mechanisms that enable ecosystem-wide standards while allowing for institution-specific implementation.

The deliverable included a maturity model for institutional AI readiness, risk assessment templates aligned to financial regulatory frameworks, and guidance on governance structures that integrate AI oversight into existing risk management processes.
